Sugal

Song by: Dionela

About this Song:

The song's title, "Sugal" (meaning "gamble" in English), immediately sets the tone for a lyrical journey filled with uncertainty and hope. Dionela's vocals convey a raw vulnerability as she sings about the emotional rollercoaster of taking a chance on a relationship.

The lyrics delve into the complexities of love, exploring themes of fear, desire, and the possibility of heartbreak. Despite the risks involved, Dionela expresses a willingness to gamble it all for the sake of love, hoping that the payoff will be worth it. The song's message resonates with anyone who has ever felt the thrill and trepidation of falling in love.
